Your task:


In this study, you will be asked to make a savings decision.
  • You will receive a budget of 100 points, each of which is worth $0.10. At the end of the study, we will convert your points earned into Dollars.
  • You need to decide how much of this amount to receive immediately and how much to save until six months from now.
  • The amount that you save will earn interest at an interest rate that will be shown to you. You will not earn interest on money you choose to receive immediately.
  • There is no risk that you won’t receive the money you save – we guarantee that the amount that you save, plus the interest it accrues, will be delivered to your account in six months. When a payment is delivered, we will also send you a reminder through Prolific to cash out the payment.
  • In total, you will complete 13 rounds of this task. Across these rounds, the interest rate varies. These rounds are completely independent from one another. If one of the rounds of this task is selected to determine your bonus, only your decision in this one round will determine your bonus.


Your bonus payment:

Your decisions in this part of the study may affect your bonus payment. If a decision in this part is selected for payment, you will immediately receive the money you don’t save, and you will receive the money you save and the interest it accrues in six months from now.

Your certainty:

In each round, we will ask you two questions:
  • How much you would like to save.
  • We will ask you how certain you are (in percentage terms) that the decision you made reflects the best decision you can take for yourself, given your personal preferences and the available information.
