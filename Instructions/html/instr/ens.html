Your task:


In this part of the study, you will decide between two hypothetical car lease options.
  • Imagine you are moving to a new city where you will be spending the next 3 years on a work contract. For your daily commute within the city as well as your other transportation needs, you have decided to lease a car for those 3 years.
  • You have narrowed down your options to two vehicles.
    Toyota 2020 Camry
    MPG: 28 city/ 39 highway
    Engine: 2.5 L 4-cylinder
    Horsepower: 203 hp
    Toyota 2023 Camry Hybrid
    MPG: 51 city/53 highway
    Engine: 2.5 L 4-cylinder
    Horsepower: 176 hp

    The Camry Hybrid model will be more expensive to lease, but will result in fuel cost savings relative to the Camry because it has a higher miles-per-gallon (MPG) rating. Over the next three years, fuel will cost $3 a gallon, on average.
  • In each round, you will be given a different scenario. In each scenario, we will tell you how many miles you expect to drive each year. You will then make a series of leasing decisions between the two vehicles above, under different assumptions on the total annual leasing costs.
  • In total, you will complete 13 rounds of this task. Across these rounds, the scenario about how many miles you expect to drive will vary. These rounds are completely independent from one another.
